## 简体中文

「行为奖励」是一款**纯本地**应用，用于家长记录与引导孩子的日常行为。

- **不采集、不上传、不共享任何个人信息。** App 不联网、无账号、无服务器、无云同步。
- 你输入的全部内容（孩子名字、头像、奖惩规则、积分流水、奖励、兑换记录）以及你选作头像的照片，**只保存在你自己的设备上**（含本地备份文件）。开发者及任何第三方都无法访问。
- **无第三方 SDK、无第三方分析、无广告、无任何追踪。**
- 选择头像照片仅用于在本机显示，不会离开你的设备。
- 家长 PIN 以加盐哈希存于设备钥匙串（Keychain），明文 PIN 不会写入磁盘。
- 删除本 App 即清除全部本地数据。
- 本 App 不面向也不刻意采集任何儿童的可识别个人信息（因为它根本不采集任何数据）。

如有疑问，请联系：xinster819ca@gmail.com

## English

"Reward Stars" is a **fully on-device** app that helps parents record and guide a child's everyday behavior.

- **It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.** No network, no account, no server, no cloud sync.
- Everything you enter (child name, avatar, reward/penalty rules, score history, rewards, redemptions) and any photo you choose as an avatar is stored **only on your device** (including local backup files). Neither the developer nor any third party can access it.
- **No third-party SDKs, no third-party analytics, no advertising, no tracking of any kind.**
- A photo chosen as an avatar is used only for on-device display and never leaves your device.
- The parent PIN is stored as a salted hash in the device Keychain; the plaintext PIN is never written to disk.
- Deleting the app removes all local data.
- The app does not target or deliberately collect any identifiable personal information from children (it collects no data at all).
